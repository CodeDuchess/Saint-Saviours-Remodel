Donate to Saint Saviour's Center


Donations from companies and individuals are the lifeblood of Saint Saviour's Center. Donations of any size are acceptable and appreciated. The center accepts donations in the form of currency, books, food, clothing and diapers. Other items not listed are not accepted. Please note that this policy ensures that we have a need for the item and that we are able to manage our growing inventory.



Donation Details

If you have a preference regarding how your monetary doantion will be spent, please share that information with us and we will ensure that your wishes are fulfilled. FOr example, you can request that you money be spent on diapers for the Wake Diaper Bank, gas and other supplies for SALT outings, items for our annual Gala's auction, etc. Saint Saviour's Center is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. It is financed through gifts from individuals, churches, foundations and building-use fees. Tax deductible contributions and in-kind donations are accepted. To donate by mail, send a check payable to: Saint Saviour's Center 616 Tucker Street Raleigh, NC 27603 For information on gifting stock or mutual funds, contact Sharon Hayes at 919-833-6400, ext. 201 or shayes@saintsaviourcenter.org. Please also call Ms. Hayes if you have good you want to donate so that drop-off can be arranged.

Real People, A Real Difference!

George and Amelia are Wake County residents who benefit from the Wake County Diaper Program. Here is what George says about his experience with Saint Saviour's Center: "Saint Saviour's Center has given me not just diapers but peace of mind that there is help when money gets tight. There is nothing quite as stressful as not being able to give your baby the necessities, like clean diapers." A small donation really can make a difference. Will you make a difference for other families like George and Amelia?
