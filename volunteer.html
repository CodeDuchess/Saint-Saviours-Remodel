Volunteer at Saint Saviour's Center

Saint Saviour's Center is a community outreach ministry that addresses largely unmet needs of low-income residents of Raleigh and Wake County by easing burdens such as aging, poverty, hunger and homelessness. Volunteers are the heartbeat of Saint Saviour's Center. Without them, many of our programs would not be able to operate. Volunteer opportunities are available for all ages and skill levels. Contact our office at 919-833-6400 to learn more.
